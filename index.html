About

Let me introduce myself.

I'm a Full Stack Web Developer currently living in Houston, TX. I'm originally from central New York and attended Rush University in Chicago for my undergraduate degree where I majored in vascular ultrasound. Upon graduation I moved to Houston, TX where I began working as a vascular sonographer for the past ten years. After many years working as a vascular sonographer, I felt the need to challenge myself more. I've always loved science and technology so web development was a perfect fit for my next endeavor. I attended Digitalcrafts coding bootcamp where I was immersed in new technologies such as React, Jquery, Node.JS, Redux, Postgres, and Javascript.
